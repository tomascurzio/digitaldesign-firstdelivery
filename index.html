This year, 2024, we're doing the first edition of the new and highly regarded Buenos Aires International Film Festival (BAIFF). We invite you to be a part of this new era of the film community in the biggest city of Argentina.

Challengers by
Luca Guadagnino

Luca Guadagnino has been making autheur cinema for years. Within his films we can find Call Me by Your Name, Bones and All, or the remake of Suspiria, and the TV Show We Are Who We Are. He now returns with the new film produced by Zendaya and written by Justin Kuritzkes, Challengers, a sport driven romance and kind of thriller where nothing is what it seems.

Sign me in... But, where?

Buenos Aires has some of the most beautiful cinemas in the world. The venues of the festival will be: Cinépolis Houssay, Cine Gaumont, and CineArte Cacodelphia. In these historical cinemas, directors, authors and producers from around the world will be presenting their new films for the broad audience.
